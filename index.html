Guru Arjan Dev

General

Guru Arjan Dev was sent to earth in the year of 1563 on April 15th in Goindval Punjab. His parents are Guru Ram Das and the daughter of Guru Amar Das. He is the fifth guru of the sikh religion (learning a religion) and he spoke Punjabi. He helped every body no matter what (He never feared death) in the future. He merged with god on 30 May 1606.

Examples Of What He Did

  1. He and the previous Gurus wrote certain banis (Recitations) for certain effects. For example Japji Sahib. An example of a person who benefitted from the effects is Vazir Khan. Vazir Khan was plagued with a unknown disease and he tried all known cures. But all failed until, one day when a started sikh reciting Sukhmami Sahib in front of him. When he stopped the disease got worse.

    The point is that bani is the cure of all suffering

  2. Completed construction of the Darbar Sahib
  3. Compiled the hymns of previous Gurus and of other saints into Adi Granth
